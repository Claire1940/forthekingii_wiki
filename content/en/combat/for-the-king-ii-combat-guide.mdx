<Section icon={Target} title="Focus and Action Economy">

Focus is your most valuable resource for mitigating RNG. It allows you to guarantee a roll, turning potential failures into successful actions. Learning when to conserve Focus and when to spend it is the hallmark of a veteran player.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify High-Risk Rolls">
    Reserve Focus for critical moments, such as landing a killing blow on a boss or escaping an ambush.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plan for Recovery">
    Use towns, camps, or specific traits like Refocus to replenish your meter before entering dungeons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimize Efficiency">
    Never hoard Focus until death; spending it to prevent a status effect or a turn skip is always worth the cost.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Resource Management">
Spend spare Focus before interacting with a Sanctum or Stone Hero. These objects often provide a full meter refill, and any Focus you had previously will be wasted if you don't use it first.
</Callout>

</Section>
