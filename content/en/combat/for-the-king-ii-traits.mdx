<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Every Trait">

Unlocking all **for the king ii traits** requires a mix of exploration, combat prowess, and quest completion. Most will naturally unlock as you play through the various chapters, but a few require specific actions within a single turn or finding hidden rooms.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Level 7 for Gifted">
    This is one of the simplest unlocks. Simply play through any chapter and ensure at least one character reaches level 7. This is the premier choice for the Hobo class.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Focus for Deep Thinker">
    To unlock Deep Thinker, you must spend a total of 8 or more Focus points in the Overworld during a single turn. This can be spread across your party members.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Score Crits for Tactician">
    This trait unlocks after repeatedly causing critical hits and high damage numbers. High-speed characters with high-crit weapons will speed up this process significantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete Chapter Quests for Eager">
    Eager is tied to progress. You must complete the quest "Deal with the Thieving Imps" in Act 1 of The Primordial Oak or complete Chapter 2 to see this in the Lore Store.
  </Step>
</Steps>
