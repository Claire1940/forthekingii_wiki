<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Weapon Scaling and Attributes">

In **For The King II**, your weaponry dictates not just how much damage you deal, but also your attack patterns, speed penalties, and tactical utility on the Battle Grid. Unlike traditional RPGs, any character can technically equip any weapon, but their success rate is tied entirely to their primary stats. 

Using a weapon that does not align with your character's highest stat is a quick way to miss crucial attacks. The table below outlines how weapon types align with core stats and their typical combat behaviors.

| Weapon Category | Primary Stat | Damage Type | Typical Grid Targeting | Key Combat Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bows & Muskets** | Awareness | Physical | Ranged / Column | High critical chance, armor pierce |
| **Swords & Axes** | Strength | Physical | Single Target / Splash | High raw damage, shield shredding |
| **Staves & Tomes** | Intelligence | Magical | Row / AOE Splash | Status ailments, resistance ignore |
| **Lutes & Instruments** | Talent | Physical/Magic | Buff Aura / Splash | Party buffs, gold multipliers, daze |
| **Daggers & Sais** | Speed / Talent | Physical | Single Target / Piercing | Fast turn recovery, high bleed chance |

<Callout type="tip" title="Accuracy vs. Raw Damage">
A weapon with fewer dice rolls (pips) is significantly more reliable than one with five or six pips. If your character has an 80% stat rating, a 2-pip weapon has a much higher chance of scoring a perfect roll than a 5-pip weapon, ensuring your special status effects trigger consistently.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Top-Tier Intelligence and Awareness Weapons">

Intelligence and Awareness weapons dominate the backline of the Battle Grid. While Intelligence weapons like Tomes and Staves focus on applying massive magical status effects across entire rows, Awareness weapons specialize in long-range physical piercing and single-target elimination.

### Best Intelligence Weapons (Magic Damage & Control)
- **Tomes of Arcane Power**: Excel at row attacks and applying Shock, Burn, or Freeze to multiple enemies simultaneously.
- **Healing Staves**: Essential for classes like the Herbalist to scale their Party Heal capabilities.
- **Wands**: Great for high-frequency single-target magic damage that ignores enemy physical armor.

### Best Awareness Weapons (Ranged Physical Damage)
- **Muskets & Blunderbusses**: Massive physical damage output, but often require a reload turn unless paired with specific traits.
- **Recurve Bows**: High speed, low pip count, and frequent Called Shot critical strikes.
- **Spears & Polearms**: Allow melee characters to strike from the back row or hit column targets.
