<Section icon={Map} title="How Stats Control Fahrul: The Dice Roll System">

In the turn-based tabletop world of *For The King II*, every action—from swinging a giant warhammer to disarming a spike trap—is determined by dice rolls. Your **for the king ii character stats** are not passive numbers; they are the direct percentage chance of success for each slot on an action's roll check. 

For instance, if a character with 78 Intelligence uses a spell that requires three slot checks, each slot has a baseline **78% chance** to succeed. Stacking your primary attributes is the single most effective way to eliminate bad luck and ensure consistent combat performance.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Character Stats Explained">

The game features seven primary attributes that dictate how your party interacts with enemies and the environment. Matching the right weapon to the right attribute is the difference between a clean victory and a wiped run.

### Physical and Defensive Attributes

*   **Strength (STR):** Governs accuracy with heavy bladed and blunt weapons. It is also tested during physical overworld challenges, such as breaking down locked doors in dungeons or pulling legendary swords from stone markers.
*   **Vitality (VIT):** Directly determines your maximum Health Points (HP) and physical endurance. High Vitality is required to survive environmental hazards like poison wells and is the primary scaling factor for defensive shield abilities.
*   **Speed (SPD):** Determines your initiative order at the start of combat and how frequently your character takes turns. Speed also dictates your success rate when sneaking past enemy camps or attempting to flee from an ambush.

### Utility and Magic Attributes

*   **Intelligence (INT):** Governs magic accuracy with staves, wands, and scrolls. High Intelligence is critical for magical damage dealers and support healers, and it controls your success rate on mental puzzles.
*   **Awareness (AWA):** Represents your character's alertness and accuracy with ranged bows, polearms, and firearms. It is highly valued for backline damage dealers who need to snipe priority targets.
*   **Talent (TAL):** Dictates your skill with musical instruments, bombs, and specialized trickster weapons. It also directly impacts boat movement speed on water tiles and your success rate when disarming overworld traps.
*   **Luck (LUK):** A static attribute that cannot be boosted by standard level-ups. Luck governs games of chance, random events, and chaotic challenges like those found in the Dark Carnival. Most classes start with a base Luck of 55.

| Attribute | Primary Weapon Types | Key Overworld Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strength** | Swords, Axes, Clubs, Hammers | Breaking doors, physical tests |
| **Vitality** | Shields, Heavy Spears | Poison resistance, HP scaling |
| **Intelligence** | Staves, Wands, Tomes | Magical puzzles, scroll use |
| **Awareness** | Bows, Hand Crossbows, Muskets | Ambush control, scouting |
| **Talent** | Lutes, Bombs, Whips, Pistols | Trap disarming, boat movement |
| **Speed** | Daggers, Rapiers, Fist Weapons | Sneaking, fleeing, turn order |

<Callout type="warning" title="Evasion vs. Speed Warning">
While Speed influences your turn order, Evasion is a separate defensive rating that can be stacked up to **100%**. Late-game evasion tanks (combining high Awareness/Speed gear with Evasion buffs) can dodge almost all incoming physical attacks, making them incredibly powerful on Master difficulty.
</Callout>

  <Step num="1" title="Inspect Loot Compatibility">

  <Step num="2" title="Stack Candies on Your Specialists">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure Biome Sanctums and Stone Heroes">
    Search each new biome for Sanctums and Stone Heroes. Sanctums grant massive character buffs and a vital revive safety net, while Stone Heroes grant instant experience points to help your attributes scale.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Focus to Bypass Bad Stats">
    If a character must roll a check on a weak attribute (such as a Blacksmith disarming a Talent-based trap), spend your Focus points. Focus guarantees successful slots, mitigating the risk of critical failures.
  </Step>
