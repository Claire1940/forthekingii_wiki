Successfully running a Hunter requires balancing overworld exploration utility with high-priority target execution in combat. Follow this step-by-step progression guide to dominate your campaign.

  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Initiative">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Control the Map with Elite Ambush">
    On the overworld map, use your Hunter to lead party movements. Utilize **Elite Ambush** to isolate single targets from larger enemy groups, allowing your party to fight asymmetric battles [wiki.gg].
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Transition to Armor-Piercing Gear">
    As you enter mid-game regions, enemies will stack physical armor. Pivot your gear toward bows or polearms that feature armor-piercing attributes or inflict bleeding effects to maintain high damage output.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize for Late-Game Critical Hits">
    In the late game, stack gear that boosts your critical hit multiplier and flat Evasion. Rely on **Called Shot** triggers to melt high-health bosses while maintaining high Evasion to dodge incoming counterattacks [wiki.gg].
  </Step>
