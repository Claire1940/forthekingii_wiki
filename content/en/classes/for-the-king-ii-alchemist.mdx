<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Alchemist Class Overview and Core Stats">

The **for the king ii alchemist** is a powerful, unlockable Intelligence-based class that bridges the gap between a traditional spellcaster and a utility support. Unlike the Scholar, who focuses on Focus recovery and consistent magic damage, the Alchemist thrives on explosive burst damage and the tactical use of consumables. In the 2026 meta, the Alchemist is considered S-Tier for high-difficulty runs due to its ability to manipulate the Battle Grid with area-of-effect (AOE) attacks.

The Alchemist's primary role is to provide magic-based pressure from the backline. Their unique passive abilities make them self-sufficient, often providing the party with high-value items that would otherwise cost significant gold in towns. When building an Alchemist, your primary focus should be Intelligence to scale damage, followed by Speed to ensure your bombs land before the enemy can act.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Battle Grid Tactics">

Combat in *For The King II* is defined by the 2x4 Battle Grid, and the **for the king ii alchemist** is designed to exploit this system. While physical classes often hit single targets, the Alchemist uses "Chemical Attacks" that can hit rows, columns, or splash onto adjacent tiles. Mastering your positioning and understanding AOE patterns is the difference between a wasted turn and a team wipe.
