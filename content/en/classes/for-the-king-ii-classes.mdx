**Blacksmith: The Iron Wall**
The Blacksmith is your primary protector. By utilizing the **Guard** ability, they can absorb damage meant for the backline. In the 2x4 grid, placing a Blacksmith in the front-center tile is the standard defensive opening.

**Hunter: The Silent Scout**
Hunters excel at picking off priority targets. Their **Called Shot** passive can turn a standard attack into a devastating critical hit. They also provide the best overworld utility with **Elite Ambush**, allowing the party to dictate when and where a fight happens.

**Scholar: The Arcane Master**
Scholars are your primary source of magical damage and crowd control. They are fragile, but their **Refocus** ability allows them to regain Focus points at the end of a turn, making them the most sustainable casters in the game.

<Callout type="warning" title="Loadout Tip">
Do not ignore the **Stablehand** and **Alchemist** once you unlock them. The Alchemist, in particular, can provide free bombs and potions, which are vital for surviving the early-game resource drought.
</Callout>
