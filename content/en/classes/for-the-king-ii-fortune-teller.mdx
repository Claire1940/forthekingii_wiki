To maximize the impact of your **for the king ii fortune teller**, follow this progression path to optimize your gear, stats, and positioning throughout your campaign run.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Starting Gear">
    Equip your default Crystal Ball and Tarot Card. Use your basic magic attacks to target enemy vulnerabilities while staying in the backline.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Initiative and Speed">
    Acquire gear that boosts Speed and Awareness. Acting early in the turn order allows you to apply Tarot buffs before your main damage dealers take action.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Transition to Mid-Game Weapons">
    Look for advanced Orbs or Bows that scale with your high base Awareness (83 on Apprentice). Keep an eye out for gear that adds passive Focus regeneration.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Defensive Layers">
    Equip middle-tier light armor that offers physical Armor without penalizing your Speed. Your goal is to reach at least 8-10 Armor by mid-game.
  </Step>
</Steps>
