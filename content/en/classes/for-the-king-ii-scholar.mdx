<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Special Abilities & Passive Skills">

The Scholar features two incredibly powerful passive abilities that scale throughout the game, providing both economic value and tactical flexibility [1]. Understanding how these passives trigger is key to maximizing the class's potential [1].

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Find Scroll">
    - **Effect**: Grants a random chance to find a magic scroll while moving on the Overworld map or exploring Dungeons [1].
    - **Scaling**: This ability scales directly off the Scholar's **Luck** stat [1].
    - **Utility**: Generates free consumables like Portal Scrolls, Identity Scrolls, or combat spells.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Refocus">
    - **Effect**: Provides an increasing chance to regain Focus points on the Overworld or inside Dungeons [1].
    - **Scaling**: This ability scales off the Scholar's **Intelligence** stat [1].
    - **Utility**: The chance to trigger increases with every missing Focus point, keeping your spellcaster fueled [1].
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="success" title="Passive Synergy">
Because **Refocus** scales off Intelligence, increasing your primary damage stat naturally improves your Focus regeneration [1]. This makes the Scholar highly self-sufficient, reducing your party's reliance on costly Golden Root consumables.
</Callout>

| Weapon Type | Primary Stat | Key Advantages | Recommended Items |

</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Party Synergy and Combat Strategy">

In *For The King II*, party composition is vital. The Scholar acts as the glass cannon of the group, requiring physical protection from sturdy frontliners [1].

### Recommended Party Compositions
- **The Classic Trio**: Scholar (Magic DPS/Support), Blacksmith (Tank/Frontline), Hunter (Physical DPS/Speed).
- **The Double-Mage Setup**: Scholar (Pure DPS), Herbalist (Healer/Group Rush), Protector (Tank).

### Key Combat Tactics
- **Exploit Weaknesses**: Use your high Intelligence to target enemies with low Magic Resistance [1].
- **Focus Management**: Do not hesitate to spend Focus on powerful AoE spells. Your **Refocus** passive will help you regain those points quickly during travel phases [1].
- **Scroll Utility**: Use the scrolls generated by **Find Scroll** to control the battlefield, teleport allies, or identify hidden dangers [1].

<Callout type="info" title="Consumable Strategy">
Always pass any found **Golden Roots** to other party members who lack passive Focus regeneration. The Scholar's **Refocus** ability makes them the lowest priority for Focus-restoring items [1].
</Callout>
