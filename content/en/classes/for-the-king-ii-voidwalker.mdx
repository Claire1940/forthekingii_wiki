<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Initial Loadout">
    Spend your Lore Store points on the **Rifle Set** if you intend to play a ranged DPS role. The Rifle provides high armor penetration, making it ideal for the Royal Mines and late-game boss encounters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assign Defensive Gear">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Focus on Status Effects">
    Look for weapons that apply **Dazed** or **Stunned**. Because the Voidwalker has high speed, they can often disable an enemy before it takes its first turn, effectively removing threats from the grid.
  </Step>
