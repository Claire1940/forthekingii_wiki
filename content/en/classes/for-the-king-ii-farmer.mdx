<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Unique Class Skill: Last Straw">

What truly sets the **for the king ii farmer** apart from other physical martial classes is their signature passive ability, **Last Straw**. This skill allows the Farmer to turn agricultural tools into tactical battlefield defenses.

### Skill Details: Last Straw
- **Trigger Mechanic**: Has a random chance to trigger at the start of the Farmer's turn during combat.
- **Effect**: Summons a **Scarecrow** onto an empty combat tile in the front or back row.
- **Scaling**: The trigger rate and effectiveness of this passive scales directly off the Farmer's **Strength**, **Vitality**, **Awareness**, and **Luck** stats.
- **Tactical Value**: The Scarecrow acts as a temporary tank, drawing enemy single-target attacks away from your party members.

  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Stat Synergy">

  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="tip" title="Synergy Tip">
