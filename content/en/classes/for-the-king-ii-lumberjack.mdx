<Section icon={Swords} title="Lumberjack Stats & Passive Skills">

The Lumberjack is a frontline specialist designed to shatter enemy defenses. Boasting exceptional Strength and high Vitality, this class excels at wielding heavy, slow two-handed weapons that deal massive single-target or row-clearing physical damage.

| Attribute | Base Value | Rating | Tactical Use |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Strength** | Very High | ★★★★★ | Primary damage scaling and weapon rolls |
| **Vitality** | High | ★★★★☆ | Increases maximum health and frontline survival |
| **Speed** | Low | ★★☆☆☆ | Delays turn order, requiring speed-boosting gear |
| **Awareness** | Moderate | ★★★☆☆ | Secondary physical utility and ambush checks |

### Passive Abilities
- **Justice**: Has a random chance to turn a standard single-target attack into a devastating splash attack that damages adjacent targets.
- **Door Slam**: Increases physical damage when attacking fortified targets, barricades, or shielded frontlines on the Battle Grid.

<Callout type="warning" title="Speed Penalty Warning">
Because the Lumberjack has low base Speed, they will often act last in the turn order. Pair them with support characters or equip speed-boosting boots to prevent fast enemies from focus-firing them down before they can swing.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combat Strategy">

Maximizing the Lumberjack's impact on the Battle Grid requires careful planning, Focus management, and target prioritization. Follow this tactical rotation to turn this slow giant into an unstoppable force.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Positioning Check">
    Before starting a fight, ensure the Lumberjack is in the front row. If caught in an ambush, use your first secondary action to move them into a slot adjacent to your tank.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Analyze Enemy Defenses">
    Identify high-armor targets. Use the Lumberjack to target enemies with low Physical Defence or use armor-piercing weapon skills to bypass heavy shields.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Spend Focus Wisely">
    Do not waste Focus on easy fights. Save your Focus points for high-damage two-handed skills that hit multiple tiles or when you need to guarantee a critical execution.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trigger Justice">
    Target the center enemy in a row. If the passive skill **Justice** triggers, your attack will splash to the left and right, dealing massive collateral damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Focus Recovery">
Use your Scholar's Refocus ability or consume Golden Root during recovery phases to keep your Lumberjack's Focus bar full before entering major dungeons.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Recommended Gear and Loadouts">

Equipping the Lumberjack with the correct weapons and utility items makes the difference between a missed swing and a one-hit kill. Focus entirely on Strength-scaling weapons and gear that mitigates their low Speed.

### Best Weapons
- **Great Axe**: High raw physical damage with reliable single-target and splash skills.
- **Woodsman's Axe**: Bypasses a portion of enemy armor and scales perfectly with base Strength.
- **Two-Handed Hammer**: Excellent for stunning targets and disrupting their turn order on the Battle Grid.

### Starting Loadout Priority
1. **Strength Trinket**: Grants an immediate boost to your primary roll success rate.
2. **Speed Boots**: Helps offset the class's low natural initiative.
3. **Panax**: Keeps the Lumberjack clean of status effects like Dazed or Stunned, which completely ruin their turn economy.
