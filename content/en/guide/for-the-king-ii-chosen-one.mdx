The **for the king ii chosen one** trait was officially introduced to Fahrul during the major free content update, *A New Age*. Unlike other traits that alter raw attributes like Strength or Speed, the Chosen One trait directly influences the background rolls that determine whether your characters' passive abilities trigger at the end of a turn or during combat.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Deep Dive: Skill Chance Mechanics & Math">

To truly appreciate the power of the **for the king ii chosen one** trait, it is essential to understand how the game calculates passive skill triggers. Passive skills—such as the Blacksmith's *Steadfast*, the Herbalist's *Find Herb*, or the Scholar's *Refocus*—do not trigger automatically; they rely on hidden math.

Most passive skills scale with a character's primary stat or their Luck stat. However, the developers have placed hard caps on how high these trigger chances can go naturally. For example, the Blacksmith's *Steadfast* skill caps at a maximum of 30% from Vitality, Armor, and Resistance scaling. 

This is where the Chosen One trait shines: **Skill Bonus is added after all other calculations, penalties, and caps are computed**.

| Passive Skill | Class | Base Trigger Mechanic | Chosen One Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Steadfast** | Blacksmith | Capped at 30% from Vitality | Raises absolute cap to 35% |
| **Mend** | Blacksmith | First check scales with Level | Guarantees 100% first-check success |
| **Find Herb** | Herbalist | Random chance on overworld turn | Significantly stabilizes herb economy |
| **Refocus** | Scholar | Random chance at combat turn end | Provides highly reliable Focus recovery |

<Callout type="info" title="Chosen One vs. Lucky Trait">
The *Lucky* trait increases your Luck stat, which indirectly boosts skills that scale with Luck. However, if you are hit with status debuffs or are using skills with hard caps, *Lucky* will not help. The *Chosen One* trait bypasses these limitations completely by adding a flat 5% to the final percentage.
</Callout>

</Section>
