### Default Control Scheme Overview
The default layout combines standard WASD camera controls with contextual mouse clicks and menu hotkeys. Unlike its predecessor, For The King II optimizes several keybinds to accommodate deeper tactical choices on the battle grid.

<Callout type="tip" title="Key Binding Update">
The end-turn shortcut has been reassigned to the **Delete (DEL)** key by default. This represents a major change from the first game's Spacebar default, helping players avoid accidental turn skips during intense battles.

If the default layout does not fit your playstyle, or if you are using a non-QWERTY keyboard layout (such as AZERTY), you can customize your controls directly in the settings. 

### How to Rebind Your Keys
Follow these simple steps to customize your layout and resolve common control issues.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Open Settings Menu">
    Press the **Esc** key during gameplay or on the main menu to open the system options, then click on **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Controls Tab">
    Click on the **Controls** tab at the top of the settings window to view the current keyboard and mouse layout.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Rebind Key Actions">
    Select the action you want to change (such as End Turn or Inspect), click the current keybinding, and press your preferred new key.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Save and Apply Changes">
    Click **Apply** at the bottom of the screen to confirm your new layout and return to your campaign in Fahrul.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Troubleshooting Controller Overlay Bugs
A common bug in For The King II causes controller button prompts (like Xbox or PlayStation icons) to display on screen even when playing with a mouse and keyboard. This can make it difficult to identify your correct PC keybinds.

<Callout type="warning" title="Fix Displayed Controls Bug">
To remove the unwanted controller layout from your display, navigate to the **Controls** tab in your Settings and toggle off **Show Footer Controls**. This forces the HUD to display standard PC keyboard prompts.
</Callout>
