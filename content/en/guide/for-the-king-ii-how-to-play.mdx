Understanding status effects is equally important. In For The King II, a single debuff can spiral into a total party wipe if not managed correctly.

| Status Effect | Mechanic | How to Cure |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dazed** | Removes next secondary action (no items) | Panax or wait 1 turn |
| **Stunned** | Skips the target's next full turn | Salty Nut or wait 1 turn |
| **Acid** | Permanently dissolves gear over time | Acid Sponge (Immediate) |
| **Bleed** | Deals damage every combat turn | Bandages or Party Heal |
| **Curse** | Massive stat penalty until removed | Hag's Bane or Town Healer |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
**Acid** is the most dangerous status effect for long-term survival. If your legendary weapon is dissolved, your run's power level drops significantly. Always carry Acid Sponges in the mid-to-late game.
</Callout>
