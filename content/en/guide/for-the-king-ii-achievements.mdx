<Callout type="tip" title="Silver Snake Strategy">
The Silver Snake in Chapter 3 and 5 has 100 Resistance to physical and magic damage but only 1 HP. Use items or abilities that deal "fixed" damage or guaranteed hits to ensure it doesn't flee before you can land a blow.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat, Scourges, and Enemy Hunting">

Combat achievements in For The King II track your long-term progress across multiple campaigns. From killing 1,000 enemies to mastering specific weapon types like firearms, these require consistent play. Additionally, the game features "Scourges"—powerful mini-bosses that haunt the world map and must be defeated to prevent global debuffs.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Scourge">
    Watch the timeline at the top of the screen. When a Scourge icon nears the active marker, their influence begins to affect Fahrul.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Stronghold">
    Scourges hide in specific locations (e.g., Lucky's Vault or the Desecrated Church). Use scouting scrolls or vision to find them on the map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prepare for the Fight">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Seal the Location">

<Section icon={Shield} title="DLC Content: Child of Chaos & Tinkerers of Fate">

The *Tinkerers of Fate* DLC and the *Child of Chaos* update introduced 18 new achievements to the game. These focus on two new classes—the Toymaker and the Fortune Teller—and a new campaign map. To earn these **for the king ii achievements**, you must engage with the new collection mechanics: Dolls and Tarot Cards.

**New Class Mechanics:**
- **The Toymaker**: Collects Dolls after killing enemies. This chance is boosted by Talent and Luck. Note that being poisoned prevents Doll drops.
- **The Fortune Teller**: Draws Tarot Cards at the end of their turn. This is influenced by Luck and Awareness.

| DLC Achievement | Type | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Family Matters** | Story | Complete Child of Chaos campaign |
| **Plangologist** | Collection | Collect 25 Dolls with the Toymaker |
| **The World** | Collection | Collect 21 Tarot Cards with the Fortune Teller |
| **Storm Chaser** | Challenge | Collect all 3 Goblets in a single run |
| **Seek and Destroy** | Challenge | Destroy all 6 Chaos Devices |
| **Cult Hero** | Secret | Rescue Doug from the Secret Room (Tower Floor 3) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Doug the Mercenary">
To unlock Doug, you must find the secret room in the second campaign of the Child of Chaos DLC. You typically need 3 Skeleton Keys to unlock all rooms in the Final Castle. Once found, he joins your party, and in a subsequent run, you can find him in a Mercenary Caravan to set him free permanently.
</Callout>
