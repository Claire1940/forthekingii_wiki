<Section icon={Target} title="Combat Grid and Status Effects">

Combat in For The King II Steam takes place on a 2x4 grid, making positioning just as important as your raw damage output [homepage-modules-2]. The front row is designed for tanks and melee fighters who can protect the backline, while the back row shields fragile spellcasters, ranged attackers, and healers [homepage-modules-2]. 

Area-of-effect (AOE) attacks can target entire rows or columns, meaning clumping your characters together can invite massive damage [homepage-modules-2]. Always preview enemy targeting lines and use movement actions to reposition your characters away from danger zones [homepage-modules-2].

Understanding status effects is also critical to preserving your party's tempo [homepage-modules-2]. Many enemies inflict debilitating conditions that can completely ruin a run if left untreated [homepage-modules-2].
