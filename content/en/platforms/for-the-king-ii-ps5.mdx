<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Console Setup & Multiplayer Connection">

Setting up a multiplayer session in **for the king ii ps5** requires following a specific sequence to avoid lobby issues or connection drops. Because the game does not support mid-game joining, your party must be fully assembled before loading into Fahrul.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize the Game Lobby">
    Launch the game on your PS5 and select **Multiplayer** from the main menu. Choose your desired adventure, then configure your room privacy, region, and optional room password.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assemble Your Party">
    Have your friends search for your room name or join directly via your PlayStation Friends List. Ensure all players have a stable internet connection and active PlayStation Plus subscriptions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assign Character Slots">
    Once all players are in the lobby, distribute the four character slots. In a two-player game, each player typically controls two heroes. In a four-player game, everyone controls one hero.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm Loadouts and Launch">
    Select your starting traits, gear, and cosmetics unlocked from the Lore Store. Once everyone flags themselves as ready, the host can launch the adventure to generate the procedural map.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Rejoining Disconnected Players">
If a player disconnects mid-run, the host must save the game, exit back to the main menu adventure selector, recreate the multiplayer lobby, let the disconnected player rejoin, and then reload the save file.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat Tactics & Battle Grid Mastery">

Combat on the PS5 is highly tactical, taking place on a **2x4 Battle Grid** where positioning directly influences buffs, penalties, and defensive capabilities. Managing your grid placement is just as important as landing your physical attacks.

### Battle Grid Positioning Rules

- **Front Row Placement:** Place high-vitality heroes like the Blacksmith here. Equipped with a shield, they can use the **Guard** skill to absorb incoming physical strikes meant for the backline.
- **Back Row Protection:** Keep fragile spellcasters and ranged damage dealers like the Scholar and Hunter in the back. This shields them from direct melee attacks, though they remain vulnerable to row and column-wide area attacks.
- **Focus Spending:** Use your Focus points to guarantee successful dice rolls on crucial combat abilities, healing spells, or crowd-control effects. Restore spent Focus by meditating in towns or consuming Golden Root.

### Status Effects and Tactical Counters

| Status Effect | Combat Penalty | Optimal Counter / Cure |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dazed** | Disables secondary actions, blocking movement and item use | Panax / Support Cleanse |
| **Stunned** | Skips the target's next turn entirely | High physical defense / Shield Guard |
| **Burn** | Deals fire damage over time; caused by burning tiles | Grid movement / Fire resistance |
| **Poison** | Deals damage over time on the overworld and lowers combat stats | Poison Cleanse / Town Healer |
| **Acid** | Permanently dissolves valuable equipped gear | Immediate removal / Avoid acid-spitting foes |

<Callout type="info" title="Environmental Hazards">
Pay close attention to the battlefield. You can use knockback skills to push enemies into lethal pools of fire or acidic hazards present on the grid, turning the environment against your opponents.
</Callout>
